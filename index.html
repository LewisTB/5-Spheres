The 5 Spheres of Life

A place where I explore a concept I have been thinking about and its applications. It's a work in progress!

Vitasphere

Health. Physical, cognitive, and emotional well-being. The foundational pillar of life.

Ergosphere

Tasks. Actions taken in the world, to achieve goals, provide maintenance or manage resources. The instrument through which we live.

Sociosphere

Relationships to significant others, family, friends and society. Interactions with others.

Curiosphere

Experience. Curiosity, learning, creativity, presence and stimulation. Higher-order human needs.

Ethicosphere

Values. Ethical, moral and spiritual matters which can be used to guide how one chooses to live. A transcendent sphere which looks beyond the self
